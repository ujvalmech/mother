The Great Story

Our Journey

Mother Fast Food & Restaurant is one of the oldest and most beloved Gujarati food providers in Surat. Since 1998, we have been dedicated to serving authentic vegetarian Gujarati cuisine. Our commitment to quality, taste, and hygiene has earned us a loyal customer base and a Google rating of 4.5 out of 5.0.

Our family-run business ensures that every meal is prepared with care and consistency, as we handle everything ourselves without involving external staff. This allows us to maintain the high standards that our customers have come to expect and appreciate.
